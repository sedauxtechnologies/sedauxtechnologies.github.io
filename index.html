About Us

At Sedaux Technologies, we combine a passion for automation with deep expertise in database and cloud infrastructure. Our name, "Sedaux," is inspired by classic Unix/Linux commands — 'sed' (stream editor) and 'aux' (auxiliary processes) — symbolizing precision, control, and powerful backend operations.

Built on the belief that efficiency and reliability drive business success, Sedaux Technologies focuses on delivering expert DB2 Database Services, High Availability (HA) Solutions, and seamless Cloud Migrations. Our mission is to simplify complex IT landscapes while ensuring enterprise-grade performance and resilience.

With years of experience and a commitment to excellence, we help businesses stay agile, secure, and future-ready.
